SON

Spatial puzzles and perspective tricks

the game

The main character, Son, moves the sun at will, projecting shadows that show fragments from a distant past. This will show old objects, or make new constructions disappear, and allow our character to progress in his quest.

This project has been developed at ENTI-UB , and the support of the Snap Creative Challenge: Reimagine the future of storytelling with augmented reality For more information, contact us.
